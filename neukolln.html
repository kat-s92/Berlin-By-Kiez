Populus Coffee

First thing's first...coffee, of course!
Imo this is the best coffee in Berlin and is definitely worth a visit for the coffee enthusiasts. Roasted right there in the back of the shop, the coffee always rastes amazing and has that tang!
A great spot in Reuterkiez to spend an afternoon people watching overlooking the Landwehrkanal.

Maybachufer 20, 12047 Berlin

La Maison

Another great cafe along the Landwehrkanal with a large comfy terrace that stays sunny into the afternoon!
La Maison serve up some great coffees, baked goods, sandwiches and even cocktails. Make sure to try the almond croissant, it is addicitively tasty and worth the queue but get there early, they sell out fast!

Paul-Lincke-Ufer 17, 10999 Berlin

Motif Wein

A combo of natural wine and music! The guys stock delicious wines, play great records and host funky events! The staff are super knowledgeable and friendly and the dimmed lights keep you there for hours into the evening.
It is also a shop, so you can buy your fav wine right there!

Weserstr. 189 12045 Berlin

Treptower Park

A huge park with a lot going on. Here you can rent a boat, watch a movie, listen to aspiring musicians, enjoy a dance or just chill by the Spree.
Insel der Jugend buried quite deep inside in the park hosts some great summer evening events and has a beach bar, perfect for watching the sunset.

Treptower Park, 12435 Berlin

Neuköllner Stoff

Located along Maybachufer Straße that stretches along the Landwehrkanal, this a weekend market where creatives of Berlin come together to display their handywork.
The market is divided into sections: fabrics, design, handicrafts, art, food.
You will find a wide variety of goods here locally made with love.

Maybachufer, 12045 Berlin

Passage Kino

If you are a fan of Berlin's vintage cinemas, you will definitely enjoy a trip to the Passage Kino. Here, you can find a mixture of art house, current Hollywood productions and European film art on the screen. Film festival screenings also take place here. The large spaces and arched windows will transprt you to the bigger days of Cinema.

Karl-Marx-Straße 131, 12043 Berlin

Klunkerkranich

Located between the areas of Schiller and Reuter Kieze, this is a roof-top bar at the last level of the Neukölln Arcaden shopping mall. You must enter through the car park at the top floor but the maze to get there is worth it! One of the best places in the city to watch the sunset with great music and performance artists.

Karl-Marx-Straße 66, 12043 Berlin

Freiluftkino and Comedy Nights in Hasenheide

Outdoor cinemas are everyone's favourite summer activity, no more words necessary! Hasenheide also hosts a Comedy Night during Summers in an intimate atmosphere, which feels more like a group of friends.

Hasenheide, 10967 Berlin
